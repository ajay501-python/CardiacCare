Clinical Studies

Our research has been validated through rigorous clinical trials at leading pediatric hospitals:

Multicenter Validation of Machine Learning for Neonatal Cardiac Arrest Prediction

2023 1,402 patients 6 centers JAMA Pediatrics
This prospective multicenter study demonstrated 91.2% sensitivity and 88.7% specificity in predicting cardiac arrest events 3.2 minutes (median) before occurrence, with a 38% reduction in unanticipated arrests at intervention sites.
Full Text (PDF) DOI: 10.1001/jamapediatrics.2023.1234

Impact of Early Warning Systems on Neurological Outcomes in Post-Arrest Neonates

2022 672 patients 4 NICUs Pediatric Research
Randomized controlled trial showing 27% improvement in survival without severe neurological disability (OR 1.73, 95% CI 1.12-2.68) when using CardiacCare system compared to standard monitoring.
Full Text (PDF) DOI: 10.1038/s41390-022-02176-8
