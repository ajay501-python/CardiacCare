Algorithm Innovations

Our patented prediction algorithm incorporates several novel approaches to neonatal cardiac arrest detection:

Adaptive Thresholding

Dynamically adjusts to individual patient baselines using continuous learning, reducing false positives by 42% compared to fixed thresholds

Multimodal Fusion

Integrates ECG, SpO2, blood pressure, and temperature data through attention mechanisms, achieving 93% cross-validation accuracy

Early Warning Score

Generates a continuously updated risk score (0-10) that correlates with clinical outcomes (AUC = 0.94 in validation studies)

Explainable AI

Provides interpretable feature importance scores to help clinicians understand prediction rationale

Real-Time Data Flow

Our system processes physiological data through a carefully designed pipeline:

1

Data Acquisition

Continuous streaming from bedside monitors at 250Hz sampling rate, with automatic quality control and artifact removal

2

Feature Extraction

Calculation of 78 time-domain and frequency-domain features every 5 seconds, including heart rate variability metrics

3

Risk Assessment

Parallel processing through our ensemble model (CNN for spatial patterns + LSTM for temporal trends + Bayesian for uncertainty)

4

Clinical Alerting

Multi-tiered notifications (dashboard highlight → nursing station alert → code blue activation) based on risk score thresholds

5

Feedback Loop

Clinician responses and outcomes are incorporated to continuously improve model performance (active learning)
